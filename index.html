NLP Deep Dive

How machines understand and generate human language.

Before a computer can "understand," it must turn words into numbers. Here are three key methods.

Bag of Words (BoW)

The simplest method. It counts word frequency, ignoring grammar and order. It's fast, but loses context.

Word Embeddings

The modern approach. It maps words to a conceptual space, where similar words are clustered together, capturing meaning and context.

TF-IDF: Finding Important Words

A smarter method that scores words based on how frequent they are in one document versus how rare they are across all documents. This helps identify key topics.
